About me

Hello! My name is Mini and I am a lifestyle features journalist based in London. I have experience writing across a range of topics - from health and nutrition trend reports to emotional, first-person reported features and celebrity interviews. I have a Master’s degree in Magazine Journalism from City, University of London and completed my undergraduate at the University of Cambridge.

Outside of journalism, I enjoy writing short stories and dreaming about a novel I’m yet to begin… I am also a huge foodie, evidence of which you can see in my (extremely small) cake business and newsletter, Buffet.

I am also learning to code, focusing on web development and languages such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, MongoDB and Python. Check out my Github for more of that - and in the meantime, please forgive any bugs on this page. It’s a work in progress!
